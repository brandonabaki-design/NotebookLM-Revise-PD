New Workspace

Gemini Now Runs Your Google Workspace

Gemini can now create documents directly in Drive, check your email, and manage your full Workspace on your behalf — drafting Docs, scanning Gmail, and organizing files without leaving the chat.

Working well with

Docs, Sheets, Gmail, Calendar, Drive file management.

Heads up: Slides is rough

Gemini still struggles with Slides — formatting breaks, layouts drift. Stick with NotebookLM for decks.

Try it

"Draft a parent email summarizing this week's lesson and save it to my Drive." Then watch Gemini do it end-to-end.

Updated Gems × NotebookLM

Gems & Attachments Now Talk to NotebookLM

Google has updated Gems and attachments so they can interact directly with your NotebookLM notebooks. A custom Gem can now pull from a curated NotebookLM source set — your textbooks, syllabi, district policy docs — and generate output grounded in those sources.

Why it matters

Gems stop hallucinating curriculum and start citing your actual sources.
