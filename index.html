New Workspace Integration

Gemini Now Runs Your Google Workspace

Gemini can now create documents directly in your Drive, check your email, and manage your full Google Workspace on your behalf — drafting Docs, scanning Gmail for that parent reply, and organizing files without ever leaving the chat.

Working well with

Google Docs, Sheets, Gmail, Calendar, and Drive file management.

Heads up: .slides files are rough

Gemini is currently struggling with Google Slides — formatting breaks, layouts drift, and edits often miss the mark. Stick with NotebookLM for slide decks until this stabilizes.

For Educators

Try: "Draft a parent email summarizing this week's lesson and save it to my Drive." Then watch Gemini do it end-to-end.

Updated Gems × NotebookLM

Gems & Attachments Now Talk to NotebookLM

Google has updated Gems and attachments so they can now interact directly with your NotebookLM notebooks. That means a custom Gem (like EduSim) can pull from a curated NotebookLM source set — your textbooks, syllabi, district policy docs — and generate output grounded in those sources.

Why it matters

Your Gems stop hallucinating curriculum and start citing your actual sources — textbook chapters, scheme of work, MOE docs.

Try it next

Build a NotebookLM with your unit's primary sources, then attach it to a planning Gem to generate lessons, quizzes, or rubrics grounded in that material.

For Educators

This finally closes the loop between your custom Gems and your trusted source library. A full training module on this workflow is coming soon.
